How It Works?

Lock and manage your ERC20 tokens on X Layer with JaceLocker!

  • Connect Your Wallet: Use MetaMask to connect to X Layer (chain ID: 196). Ensure enough OKB for gas fees.
  • Approve Tokens: Enter a valid ERC20 token address and approve the contract to manage your tokens.
  • Lock Tokens: Specify the amount and lock duration (in days), then lock your tokens securely.
  • Unlock Tokens: Select a locked token and lock ID to unlock tokens after the lock period expires.
  • View Locks: Check your locked tokens, amounts, and unlock times in the table below.
