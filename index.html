How It Works?

Lock and manage your ERC20 tokens on X Layer with JaceLocker!

  • Connect Your Wallet: Use MetaMask to connect to X Layer (chain ID: 196). Ensure enough OKB for gas fees.
  • Check Balance: Enter a valid ERC20 token address to see your balance.
  • Lock Tokens: Specify the amount and lock duration (in days), then click "Lock Token" to lock. Authorization is handled automatically if needed.
  • Unlock Tokens: Select a locked token and lock ID to unlock tokens after the lock period expires.
  • View Locks: Enter a token address and click "Load Locked Tokens" to view your locks (page 0, size 1000).
